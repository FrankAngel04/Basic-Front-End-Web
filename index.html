California

A State in the USA

Welcome to California. It is the third largest state by area and the most populated in the United States. Powered by leading industries in technology to agriculture, California is the fifth-largest economy globally. Home to over 39 million people and welcoming more than 65 million visitors annually, California is a place of extraordinary scenic landscapes. From the rugged Sierra Nevada mountains to the stretches of the Pacific coast, the geography of California is as diverse as the population. With challenges like natural disasters, and the state remains resilient, always working towards recovery and better preparedness. Over 230 state government agencies are committed to serving the people, ensuring that California remains a wonderful place to live, work, and visit. Welcome to the Golden State.
