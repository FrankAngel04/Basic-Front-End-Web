Anaheim

A City in California

City's population and year it was incorporated

Anaheim is a city located in northern Orange County, California, part of the Greater Los Angeles area in Southern California. The city has a population of 343,654 from the 2020 census, making it the most populous city in Orange County and the tenth-most populous city in California. The city was founded in 1857 and officially incorporated in 1876.

Region of the state in which the City is located and Classification

Anaheim is classified as an urban area due to its significant population density in the Southern California region.

Average income level of the City

The average income level in Anaheim are approximately $41,363. This figure represents the general income level of the city's residents.

Top three attractions in the City

Here are some notable attractions to visit in Anaheim:

  • Disneyland Resort: The iconic theme park that features two parks including Disneyland Park and Disney California Adventure.
  • Angel Stadium: A major league baseball stadium in the central hub of Anaheim, home to the Los Angeles Angels baseball team.
  • Anaheim Packing District: A food hub located in a historic part of Anaheim, offering a diverse selection of gourmet food and beverage.
